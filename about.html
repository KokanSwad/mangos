About Kokan Swad

Kokan Swad by Jadhav Bandhu is a farmer-first mango brand from the Konkan region. Our mission is to deliver naturally ripened, chemical-free Alphonso mangoes directly from our farms to customers.

From **शेतकरी ते ग्राहक**, we ensure purity, freshness, and trust in every mango. Our mangoes are grown using traditional farming practices and packed with love.
